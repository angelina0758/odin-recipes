Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with layers of spiced chicken and basmati rice. It's a staple in Indian cuisine and known for its aromatic spices and rich taste.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Salt and oil/ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done and drain.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, and ginger-garlic paste for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions until golden. Set aside half for layering.
  4. Cook marinated chicken with tomatoes and spices until done.
  5. Layer rice and chicken in a pot. Top with fried onions and herbs.

  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
