Cheese Omelette

Description

This cheese omelette is a quick and delicious breakfast option, made with fluffy eggs and gooey melted cheese. Perfectly golden on the outside and soft on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional)
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk well.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook gently without stirring.
  4. Once nearly set, sprinkle cheese evenly on top.
  5. Fold the omelette in half and cook another minute until cheese melts.
  6. Serve hot with toast or salad.
