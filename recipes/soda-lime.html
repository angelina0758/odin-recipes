Lime Soda

Description

Lime Soda is a refreshing and fizzy summer drink made with fresh lime juice, soda water, and a touch of sugar or salt. It's perfect to cool down on hot days.

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh lime
  • 1â€“2 tsp sugar (or salt, for a salty version)
  • 1 cup chilled soda water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Squeeze lime juice into a glass.
  2. Add sugar or salt and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add ice cubes and top with soda water.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves if desired.
  5. Serve immediately while fizzy.
